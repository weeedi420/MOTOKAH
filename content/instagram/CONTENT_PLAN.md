# Motokah — Instagram Content Plan (East Africa, Full Year)

**Brand line:** Africa's first truly pan-African car marketplace.
**Handle:** @motokahafrica
**Markets:** Tanzania 🇹🇿 · Kenya 🇰🇪 · Uganda 🇺🇬 · Rwanda 🇷🇼 · Burundi 🇧🇮 · Ethiopia 🇪🇹

---

## 1. Languages & voice

Lead in **English + Swahili** (every post — Swahili is the East African lingua franca). Add a localized line for the country a post targets:
- 🇹🇿🇰🇪🇺🇬 — Swahili ("Karibu Motokah", "Gari yako, bei yako")
- 🇷🇼🇧🇮 — French / Kinyarwanda touch ("Bienvenue", "Imodoka yawe")
- 🇪🇹 — Amharic touch ("እንኳን ደህና መጡ")

Voice: confident, warm, proudly African, plain-spoken. No corporate stiffness. Talk like a trusted friend who knows cars. Mix English + Swahili naturally (code-switch) the way East Africans actually speak.

## 2. Content pillars (rotate)

| Pillar | % | What |
|--------|---|------|
| 🚗 Listings / Featured cars | 35% | Real cars from the marketplace — daily hot deals, dealer spotlights, price drops |
| 💡 Buyer/Seller education | 20% | How to inspect, import duty tips, financing, avoiding scams, paperwork per country |
| 🌍 Culture / community | 20% | Holidays, local events, road-trip routes, "car of the week from [city]" |
| 🏷️ Brand / trust | 15% | Who we are, why pan-African, verified dealers, customer stories |
| 🎉 Promotions / CTAs | 10% | List free, dealer onboarding, app download, referral |

## 3. Weekly cadence (repeatable skeleton)

- **Monday** — *Motokah Monday*: featured car + price. "Start your week with a new whip."
- **Tuesday** — *Tip Tuesday* (Kidokezo): buyer/seller education.
- **Wednesday** — *Whip Wednesday*: showroom/dealer spotlight (use scraped dealer cars).
- **Thursday** — *Throwback / Classic*: iconic cars East Africa loves (Land Cruiser, Vitz, Premio).
- **Friday** — *Feature Friday*: hot deals carousel (5–10 real listings).
- **Saturday** — *Showroom Saturday*: dealer of the week + their inventory.
- **Sunday** — *Sunday Drive*: lifestyle/road-trip/scenery + a fitting car ("Zanzibar weekend? This RAV4.").

Target: 1 feed post/day + 2–3 stories. Reels 3×/week (car walkarounds from real photos/clips).

## 4. Full-year calendar — key dates & angles

Dates are recurring anchors. Islamic dates (Ramadan/Eid) shift yearly — confirm each year.

### January
- **Jan 1 — New Year**: "New year, new ride." Resolution = upgrade your car. (All markets)
- **Jan 12 — Zanzibar Revolution Day** 🇹🇿
- **Back-to-school / new-year-budget**: affordable family cars carousel.

### February
- **Feb 14 — Valentine's**: "Treat your better half to a smoother ride." Couple road-trip cars.
- Mid-month: import-duty explainer (TZ vs KE vs UG) — high-save educational.

### March
- **Mar 8 — International Women's Day**: women drivers / female dealers spotlight.
- **Ramadan begins** (approx): respectful "Ramadan Kareem", calm tone, family + 7-seater focus.
- Easter build-up begins (travel season).

### April
- **Easter (Good Friday → Easter Monday)**: travel = road trips. Family SUV/van carousel.
- **Apr 26 — Union Day** 🇹🇿 (Tanzania + Zanzibar union).
- **Eid al-Fitr** (approx): "Eid Mubarak" + Eid gifting angle.

### May
- **May 1 — Labour Day / Mfanyakazi**: "Hard work deserves a good ride." Pickups/work vehicles.
- Mid-May: financing & instalment explainer.

### June
- **Jun 1 — Madaraka Day** 🇰🇪
- **Jun 3 — Martyrs' Day** 🇺🇬
- **Father's Day** (3rd Sun): "Gari kwa Baba." Dad-approved rides.
- **Eid al-Adha** (approx): "Eid Mubarak."

### July
- **Jul 7 — Saba Saba** 🇹🇿 (industry/trade day) — marketplace pride post.
- **Jul 1 — Independence Day** 🇧🇮 Burundi.
- **Jul 4 — Independence Day** 🇷🇼 Rwanda (Liberation Day).
- Mid-winter (cool season) — diesel/4x4 highland-drive content.

### August
- **Aug 8 — Nane Nane** 🇹🇿 (farmers' day): pickups, Land Cruisers, "gari ya shamba".
- Used-car-import season tips (Japan auction explainer).

### September
- **Sep 11 — Enkutatash / Ethiopian New Year** 🇪🇹: "መልካም አዲስ ዓመት" + Ethiopia spotlight.
- Back-to-school round 2 (some calendars).

### October
- **Oct 9 — Independence Day** 🇺🇬
- **Oct 14 — Mwalimu Nyerere Day** 🇹🇿
- **Oct 20 — Mashujaa Day** 🇰🇪 (heroes): "Heroes drive..." community stories.

### November
- **Black Friday (last Fri)**: biggest sale push — "Bei Poa Black Friday." Dealer deal carousels, countdown stories.
- Long-rains-ending → "rainy season ready" tyres/4x4 content.

### December
- **Dec 9 — Independence Day** 🇹🇿
- **Dec 12 — Jamhuri Day** 🇰🇪
- **Dec 25 — Christmas / Krismasi**: family-time, gifting, "park a bow on it."
- **Dec 26 — Boxing Day**: post-Christmas deals.
- **Dec 31 — New Year's Eve**: year-in-review (cars sold, dealers joined, countries served) + countdown.

## 6. Production notes (visuals)

- **Use real car photos** from the marketplace: scraped dealer images in `public/instagram-cars/{username}/` + jiji catalog images. Never fake stock unless a brand/illustration post.
- Square 1080×1080 feed, 1080×1920 stories/reels.
- Consistent template: Motokah logo top-left, price tag bottom-right, city + year badge, brand gold/dark theme (match site).
- Carousels: slide 1 = hook + hero car, slides 2–N = listings, last slide = CTA "Tap link in bio → Motokah".
- Reels: 6–10s walkaround from dealer photo sets, trending Bongo/Afrobeats audio, on-screen specs.
